About SiteQom

SiteQom was founded with a vision to create digital experiences that make a meaningful impact for businesses. Our name combines "Site" with the Moroccan Darija suffix "Qom," meaning "yours" - reflecting our philosophy that we don't just build websites, we create digital homes that truly belong to our clients.

We balance technical excellence with creative design, crafting websites that are both visually compelling and functionally powerful. Our approach merges clean, minimalist aesthetics with robust functionality to deliver solutions that truly perform.

Based in Morocco, we specialize in developing custom websites and digital solutions for businesses seeking to establish or strengthen their online presence. Our results-driven methodology ensures that every project we undertake is strategically aligned with our clients' business objectives and designed to deliver measurable outcomes. At SiteQom, we believe that effective digital experiences should be both beautiful and purposeful - creating seamless interactions that help businesses connect with their audiences and achieve their goals.

Our Process

How we transform your vision into digital reality

1

Discovery

We begin by thoroughly understanding your business, goals, target audience, and project requirements. This crucial first step allows us to create a strategic foundation for your project.

What happens in this phase:

  • Initial consultation and requirements gathering
  • Business objectives analysis
  • Target audience identification
  • Competitive landscape review
  • Project scope and timeline definition

Phase Outcome:

A comprehensive project brief and strategic roadmap that aligns your business goals with the website's objectives.

2

Design

With a solid understanding of your needs, we create wireframes and design concepts that bring your vision to life while adhering to best practices in user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design.

What happens in this phase:

  • Site architecture and user flow planning
  • Wireframing key page layouts
  • Visual design concept development
  • Brand integration and consistency check
  • Mobile responsiveness planning
  • Client review and feedback incorporation

Phase Outcome:

Approved design mockups for all key pages, with a clear visual direction and user experience strategy.

3

Development

Our development team transforms the approved designs into a fully functional website, focusing on clean code, optimization, and implementing all required features and functionality.

What happens in this phase:

  • Frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Backend development and database setup (if required)
  • Content Management System implementation
  • Responsive development for all device sizes
  • Integration of third-party services
  • Performance optimization
  • Testing across browsers and devices

Phase Outcome:

A fully developed, responsive website with all agreed-upon functionality implemented and tested.

4

Launch

After thorough testing and quality assurance, we deploy your website to its live environment, ensuring everything functions correctly and all final details are addressed.

What happens in this phase:

  • Final quality assurance and testing
  • Content population and review
  • SEO optimization implementation
  • Domain and hosting setup (if needed)
  • Website deployment to live server
  • Post-launch testing

Phase Outcome:

A successfully launched website that's ready to serve your business goals and impress your visitors.

5

Support

Our relationship doesn't end at launch. We provide ongoing support, maintenance, and optimization to ensure your website continues to perform optimally and evolve with your business.

What happens in this phase:

  • Regular security updates and maintenance
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Content updates as needed
  • Feature enhancements and additions
  • Analytics review and recommendations
  • Technical support

Phase Outcome:

A continuously optimized website that remains secure, up-to-date, and aligned with your evolving business needs.
